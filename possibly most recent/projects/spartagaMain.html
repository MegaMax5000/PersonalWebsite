Spartaga

2016-2017

Developer, Designer

Project Summary

Spartaga is a virtual reality Bullet-Hell action arcade game inspired by titles such as Galaga, Ikaruga, and Geometry Wars. Our goal with Spartaga was to reimagine and redefine the Bullet-Hell/Shoot-em-up genre in VR. Spartaga released in August 2017 on Steam.

My Role

With just a two person full time team, I got to dig into a wide variety of disciplines in the game development process. To name a few: Concept design, gameplay design, prototyping game mechanics, developing game mechanics, UI design and implementation, prototyping 3d enemy models, environment design, designing and developing enemy and Boss AI, level design, level architecture, achievement architecture, writing lots of other smaller utility classes and functions, and even marketing towards the end of the project. All in all, I am very happy to have worked at House of How right out of college. Not only did I get tons of developer and designer specific experience, but I also have a much better understanding of the game development process. Jumpstarting my career with this experience has been invaluable to me as a technical designer.
