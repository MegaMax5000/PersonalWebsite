Bellhapp

2016

UI Designer, UX Designer, UX Researcher, Branding

Project Summary

Bellhapp was restaurant mobile technology startup designed to link customers to restaurants through web portals to improve customer's experience. The company's mobile application facilitates menu viewing and food ordering, payment via phone, communication with restaurant staff as well as image-rich menu, specials and pairing recommendations viewing, enabling customers to receive an ecosystem that links them to the waitstaff and management team faster.

My Role

I was a design and research intern whos responsibilities include designing for mobile, tablet, and desktop views from sketch to mockup and researching the users we were designing for.
