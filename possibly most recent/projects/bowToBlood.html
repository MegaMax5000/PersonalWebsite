Bow To Blood

2018

QA, UI Designer, UI Developer, Crossplatform Companion App

Project Summary

Become a Captain! Fly your ship and command your crew as you compete to become Champion in a winner-takes-all reality show. Build relationships and plot betrayals to avoid getting voted off the season and become the last captain standing. Optional VR support.

My Role

Initially I did some QA work, then I shifted to doing some UI design and development for the Captain's Quarters interface. Once that was done, I worked on a Unity-Noesis based companion app for the game that allowed for you to help or hinder your friends or favorite streamer.'
