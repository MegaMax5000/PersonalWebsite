My Role

When I first started at Bellhapp I was a design and research intern whos responsibilities include designing for mobile, tablet, and desktop views from sketch to mockup and researching the users we were designing for. After a couple of months of working as a research and design intern, I learned that Bellhapp was competing in the Business Plan Competition (BPC) at the University of Washington and I wanted in. One of the developers and I were promoted to Developer Lead and UX Lead for the purposes of the BPC, but these titles stuck afterwards. My main role in the BPC was designing documents and pitching to hundreds of investors in the Investment Round. The document design was only a minor shift from web and mobile design, and you can see a one page summary of Bellhapp that I designed and created the layout for below. You can also find more info about Bellhapp at http://bellhapp.com/
