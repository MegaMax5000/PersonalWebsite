Virtual Phone

UX/UI Designer, Visual Designer

This project is still under construction!

I've been spending a lot of time in the Vive lately and realising what a hassle it is to take the headset off and put it back on every time I want to check a mobile notification. I saw that HTC created a way to check messages from the Vive, but thought it was lacking in a few areas. The biggest problem for me was that it intruded on the VR experience more than it should. I wanted a way to quickly check messages in-game or in-experience rather than having to go to the messaging app every time I wanted to quickly check. I also wanted to be able to receive more than just text messages or phonecalls, which the HTC app did not provide. Most of all I thought this would be a fun UX and Visual Design exercise, so I decided to spend some time on it!


I hope to have this project finished and up on the website by Sunday October 3rd at the latest! Until then, you can see some of my brainstorming sketches below.
