My Role

House of How has been working with Minecraft since 2018 in a variety of ways. The biggest aspect of the business is content creation for the Minecraft Marketplace, which allows for very rapid development processes. In the time I've worked on the Marketplace, I've been able to see 60+ games from idea to ship and acted as product manager on over 50 (in addition to my Technical Designer role). The types of games range from multiplayer minigames to narrative rpgs. The Marketplace has been successful enough for us to open a studio in sweden that is largely dedicated to the Marketplace. There are also Non-Marketplace projects and lots of internal tooling development we worked on I cannot talk about.
