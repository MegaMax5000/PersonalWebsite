Lunar Lander Game

C# Game Developer, 3D modeling with Maya, Animation, Design

Motivation:

Do you remember playing all of those Lunar Lander games as a kid? If not, give this a play and it should refresh your memory. I had made a lot of half finished games before this, so I wanted to pick a simple idea and game-style that I could take completely through the development process on my own.

Design:

This was one of the first games I made with unity. Everything in this was made by me including objects (made in blender and photoshop). It's not much compared to what I can do in unity now, but I think it is fun to remember where I started!

The game can be found at: http://www.kongregate.com/games/Aleedalee/the-1-000-000th-lunar-landing-game

Controls: WASD to move. Don’t go too far away from the map or you blow up. Don’t land on the landing pad too hard or you blow up.

There are 4 levels, have fun with that last one!
